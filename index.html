Welcome to Local Player

Your entire music library, stored securely inside your browser cache. Zero uploads required.

Quick Start

  • Click the + next to Library Sources to load a local folder or files.
  • Hover over a song and click the trash icon to remove it from your library or playlist.
  • Due to browser security, you must click ↻ Restore Session when refreshing the page.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Spacebar: Play / Pause
  • Ctrl + Right Arrow: Next Track
  • Ctrl + Left Arrow: Previous Track
  • Shift + Right/Left Arrow: Seek forward/backward 5 seconds
  • M: Mute / Unmute
